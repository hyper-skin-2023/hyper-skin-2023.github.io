    • Open Credentialized Access

    To request access to the dataset, interested researchers must follow the credentialization procedures. This includes digitally signing the End User License Agreement (EULA) with their official affiliation, acknowledging and agreeing to the terms and conditions stated in the agreement. Furthermore, researchers are expected to demonstrate their understanding of the ethical considerations associated with human-related research when accessing the data.

    Please carefully review the terms and regulations outlined in the EULA below. After reviewing the EULA, please fill out the form to request access to the dataset. Once the form is completed and submitted, interested users will be granted access to the dataset. The dataset will be provided through a secure link, which will be sent to the email address provided by the user within 24 hours.


    Data Privacy and Security

    Note that the Hyper-Skin dataset has undergone a de-identification process to adhere to the research ethics policy established by the university's research ethics board. This process involves the removal of any sensitive information that could potentially identify the participants. As a result, the dataset is entirely anonymized and stored in a secure, password-protected database.
